## Voice and visual direction

- Tone: direct, confident, technically credible, growth-oriented, and outcome-led;
  official pages use short declarative statements and measurable outcomes.
- Message hierarchy: connect the journey, show the measurement, then make the
  next action obvious.
- Palette/logo: the official site’s current visual system and logo should be
  treated as reference only until Branch confirms brand-guideline access and
  reuse rights. No official assets were downloaded.
- Existing authorized assets: none supplied; ownership/reuse permission is
  pending.
- Proposed art direction: high-contrast editorial layouts with restrained
  product/flow diagrams, abstract connected-path imagery, and real customer or
  team photography only when authorized. Avoid fabricated dashboards, logos,
  testimonials, badges, or performance evidence.
